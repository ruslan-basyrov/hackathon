# UNIQA Private Doctor (Privatarzt) — In-Scope Product Reference

> **Scope:** This document covers ONLY the Privatarzt ("Bei Arztbesuchen") path for "Myself only" (insurance for yourself). Krankenhaus (hospital) path, "Other persons," and advisor-required tariffs (Opt. Plus, Premium) are documented only as context — they route to an advisor and are **not coached**.

**Sources:** Überblick.pdf, QAYC S 2025 (Start, EN+DE), QAYC O 2025 (Optimal, EN+DE), QAYC OP 2025 (Optimal Plus, EN+DE), QAYC P 2025 (Premium, EN+DE), QAZ FIT 2025 (Fit Feeling), QAZ MW 2025 (Growing Mentally), QAZ EW 2025 (Becoming Parents), and one-page summary sheets (169–175).
**Tariff year:** 2025 | **Effective date for new features:** 12 September 2025 | **Premium overview date:** July 2025

### 2.2 Detailed Coverage Descriptions

**Category 1 — Doctor & specialist costs (all tariffs):**
- General practitioner and specialist costs — conventional medicine AND alternative medicine (excl. dentists)
- Telemedicine and video consultations
- Parent-child checkup examinations (Austrian "Eltern-Kind-Pass")
- Physician-prescribed outpatient examinations:
  - Imaging diagnostics (X-ray, CT, MRI, medical ultrasound)
  - Laboratory diagnostics (blood tests, urine tests)
  - Endoscopic diagnostics (gastroscopy)
  - Histological diagnostics (punch biopsy)
  - Cytological diagnostics (smear test)
- Ambulatory (outpatient) surgical procedures — excluding refractive eye surgery
- Preventive checkups: urological, gynecological, ophthalmological, dermatological

**Category 2 — Medications & vaccinations (all tariffs):**
- Registered pharmaceuticals
- Homeopathic remedies
- Prescription fees — 100% of costs with Austrian statutory health insurance. This also applies to medications whose price does not exceed the prescription fee.
- Vaccinations (serum + doctor's fee), including travel vaccinations

**Category 3 — Therapies & treatments (Optimal and above only):**
- Physician-prescribed treatments (conventional & alternative medicine):
  - Physiotherapists
  - Music therapists
  - Dieticians
  - Occupational therapists
  - Speech therapists
  - Orthoptists
  - Medical massage therapists
  - Healing massage therapists
  - Osteopaths
- Physician-prescribed psychotherapeutic treatments & diagnostics:
  - Clinical psychologists
  - Health psychologists
  - Psychotherapists

**Category 4 — Medical aids & visual aids (Optimal and above only):**
- Physician-prescribed aids and appliances (medical devices)
- Visual aids (glasses and contact lenses), or refractive eye surgery — per 2 calendar years from initial commencement
- **NEW (Sep 2025):** Additional refractive eye surgery coverage — double the base amount, also per 2 calendar years

### 2.3 Critical Differences for the Conversion Coach

**Start vs. Optimal — the primary upgrade decision:**

| What Start lacks | What Optimal adds | Monthly cost difference |
|---|---|---|
| Therapies & treatments (physiotherapy, psychotherapy, etc.) | €560/year for therapies | — |
| Medical aids, visual aids, refractive eye surgery | €280/year (+ €280 additional for refractive eye surgery per 2 years) | — |
| Higher medication coverage | €280 → €560/year | — |
| Higher doctor coverage | €1,120 → €1,400/year | — |
| **Total additional coverage** | **+€1,400/year** | **~€30/month (age 27)** |

**Coach argument:** "For about €1/day more, Optimal gives you therapy coverage, medical aids, and twice the medication coverage."

**Start has significant gaps — users need to know:**
- No physiotherapy, no psychotherapy, no occupational therapy
- No medical aids (crutches, bandages, etc.)
- No glasses/contact lenses coverage
- No refractive eye surgery coverage
- Medications capped at €280/year (vs. €560 for Optimal)

**Opt. Plus and Premium — visible but not online-purchasable:**
- Users clicking these see "advisory required" — the #1 frustration driver at Step 4 (66% drop-off)
- Coach should: transparently explain these require a consultation call, then highlight what Optimal already covers
- Key differentiator: Opt. Plus adds more therapy/medication/aid budget, Premium adds much more — but both need advisor

## 3. What's NOT Covered (All Tariffs)

| Exclusion | Details |
|---|---|
| Dental/oral surgery | Dental x-rays, prosthetics, orthodontics — none of the 4 tariffs cover this |
| Cosmetic/aesthetic procedures | Explicitly listed: abdominoplasty, breast augmentation/reduction/lift, liposuction, rhinoplasty, facelift, etc. — unless to remedy accident consequences |
| Strength training / medical training | Even under medical supervision — not covered |
| Memberships, subscriptions, monthly fees | Gym memberships, etc. — not covered |
| Psychotherapy (standalone) | Note: clinical psychology and health psychology ARE covered under Optimal+ (Category 3), but standalone psychotherapy sessions are NOT |
| Alcohol/substance-related conditions | Conditions caused or worsened by abuse of alcohol or drugs, and withdrawal treatments |
| Deductibles from hospital/Sonderklasse tariffs | Never reimbursed by Privatarzt tariffs |
| Combined reimbursement cap | Total reimbursement from statutory + private insurance may not exceed 100% of costs |

## 4. Reimbursement Rules

### 4.1 Reimbursement Rates

| Scenario | Reimbursement rate |
|---|---|
| You have Austrian statutory health insurance (e.g., ÖGK) and it covers part | **100% of remaining costs** (total cost minus statutory insurance share) |
| You have statutory insurance but it covers nothing for this service | **80% of costs** |
| You have no statutory health insurance | **80% of costs** |

**Coach note:** "You get up to 100% back if your statutory insurance pays their share, or 80% if they don't cover it. Total reimbursement never exceeds your actual costs."

### 4.2 How to Submit Claims

1. **If statutory insurance pays first:** Send the statutory insurance reimbursement statement + copy of invoice to UNIQA via myUNIQA app/portal or by post
2. **If you submit to UNIQA first:** You cannot then submit the same invoice to statutory insurance afterward
3. **Simultaneous submission** to both statutory insurance and UNIQA is **not permitted**
4. **Channels:** myUNIQA app/portal (photograph/scan invoices), or post to UNIQA Business Center 607, 1000 Vienna
5. **Deadline:** Claims expire after **3 years** — submit promptly, especially thermal-paper receipts (pharmacies)
6. **Per person:** Submit invoices separately for each insured person
7. **LARA doctor network:** Find doctors and book appointments at lara.uniqa.at — voluntary, free choice of doctor remains unchanged

### 4.3 Maximum Reimbursement Rule

Combined reimbursements from statutory health insurance + UNIQA Privatarzt **cannot exceed 100% of actual costs.** You cannot profit from insurance.

**Basis for calculation:** Insurance benefits are calculated based on costs **before** deduction of statutory health insurance benefits (i.e., the gross cost, not the net).

## 5. Add-On Tariffs (Combinable with All Base Tariffs)

Three supplementary tariffs can be combined with any Privatarzt base tariff (Start, Optimal, Opt. Plus, or Premium). They appear as optional extras during the purchase flow and may be online-purchasable with Start/Optimal.

| Add-on | Tariff code | Annual maximum | Reimbursement rate | Minimum combination | Min. insurance duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Fit Feeling** | QAZ FIT 2025 | €280/year | 80% of costs | Any Privatarzt base tariff | 3 years |
| **Growing Mentally** | QAZ MW 2025 | €420/year | 80% of costs | Any Privatarzt base tariff | 3 years |
| **Becoming Parents** | QAZ EW 2025 | €280/year | 80% of costs | Any Privatarzt base tariff | 3 years |

**Important:** If the base tariff ends, the add-on also ends. Add-ons cannot exist standalone.

### 5.1 Fit Feeling (QAZ FIT 2025) — €17.17/month

**What's covered (80% of costs, up to €280/year total):**

| Category | Coverage details | Sub-limit |
|---|---|---|
| Dietary supplements | From pharmacies or medical dispensaries | — |
| Massages | By regulated trades (commercial massage, Shiatsu) | — |
| Alternative treatments | By free trades (incl. alternative practitioners / Heilpraktiker) | — |
| At-home lab diagnostic tests | Self-testing kits | — |
| Podology | Foot care | — |
| Back school | Medical training | — |
| Smoking cessation programs | — | — |
| **Fitness centers and classes** | On-site or online | **€100/year sub-limit** |

**Not covered:** Dental/oral surgery, cosmetic procedures, alcohol/substance-related conditions, memberships/subscriptions, strength training, medical training.

**Coach note:** "Fit Feeling adds €17.17/month for up to €280/year in wellness benefits — fitness, massage, supplements. The fitness sub-limit is €100/year, everything else shares the remaining €180."

### 5.2 Growing Mentally (QAZ MW 2025) — annual max €420

**What's covered (80% of costs, up to €420/year total):**

Life & social counseling (Beratung, Coaching, Counselling, Betreuung) in:
- Personal development
- Self-discovery
- Problem solving
- Improving relationship skills
- Professional supervision
- **Psychosocial counseling** — with the **explicit exclusion of psychotherapy**

**Critical distinction for the Coach:** This add-on covers life coaching and psychosocial counseling, but **NOT psychotherapy**. If a user expects therapy coverage, this is not it. Psychotherapy is covered under the base Optimal+ tariff (Category 3), not this add-on.

**Not covered:** Psychotherapy, dental/oral surgery, cosmetic procedures, alcohol/substance-related conditions.

### 5.3 Becoming Parents (QAZ EW 2025) — annual max €280

**What's covered (80% of costs, up to €280/year total):**

| Category | Coverage details |
|---|---|
| Pregnancy tests | — |
| Outpatient midwife services | Care, counseling, support during birth, maternity & infant care |
| Breastfeeding counseling & postnatal exercise | — |
| Family support (first 8 weeks after birth) | Cleaning, meals, childcare — by Austrian health/social service providers or Dienstleistungsscheck |
| Nutritional counseling | By regulated trade |
| Outpatient treatment of chronic wounds | — |

**⚠️ Critical condition:** Benefits under "Family planning" (midwife services, breastfeeding counseling, postnatal exercise, family support) are **only paid if this add-on was already in force when pregnancy began.** If purchased after becoming pregnant, family planning benefits are not available. Only the insured person who is a parent of the unborn/newborn child qualifies for family planning benefits.

**Coach note:** Users thinking about starting a family need to know: buy this **before** getting pregnant, or the family planning benefits won't apply.

## 8. Upgrade / Conversion Rights (Facilitated Conversion)

This is a **critical Coach argument** for users hesitating between Start and Optimal, or wanting Opt. Plus/Premium:

| From | Can upgrade to | Can downgrade to | Conditions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Start | Optimal | — (lowest tier) | Facilitated: no health re-assessment, no new waiting periods |
| Optimal | Opt. Plus | Start | Facilitated: same conditions |
| Opt. Plus | Premium | Optimal | Facilitated: same conditions |
| Premium | — (highest tier) | Opt. Plus, Optimal, or Start | Facilitated: same conditions |

**Conditions for facilitated conversion (all tariffs):**
1. Insured person has **not yet reached age 65** at the next renewal date
2. Insurance coverage must have been in force for **at least 3 years** at the time of conversion
3. Insured must be covered at the **most recent adjustment level** (not under a substitute tariff following rejection of a premium adjustment)
4. Conversion request must be received **at least 1 month** before the next renewal date
5. **Minimum insurance duration after conversion:** 3 years

**Key Coach messages:**
- "Not sure about Optimal? Start with Start — you can upgrade to Optimal after 3 years without a new health check."
- "Want Opt. Plus or Premium? You can start with Optimal online now and upgrade to Opt. Plus after 3 years, no health re-assessment needed."
- "Premium increases with age at the time of upgrade — but your health status is never re-evaluated."

## 9. Option (Waiver of Premium) — Supplementary Coverage

Available for all base tariffs as an optional add-on:

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Premium surcharge | 15% of the monthly base tariff premium |
| What it does | If you become unable to work (illness/disability), the option covers your insurance premium payments so your coverage continues |
| Coverage during option period | **None** — no benefits are paid while the option is active, it only pays your premiums |
| Maximum entry age | 35 |
| Reactivation | Annually at renewal date, up to age 50 |
| Minimum insurance duration after purchase | 3 years |

**Coach note:** This is a "premium waiver" — it doesn't pay for medical treatment, it pays your monthly Privatarzt premiums if you can't work. It's a separate financial protection product.

## 13. Key Product Facts for the Coach

### Quick Reference: What Start Covers vs. Doesn't Cover

**Start DOES cover (up to €1,120/year for medical, €280/year for medications):**
✅ Doctor & specialist visits (conventional + alternative medicine)
✅ Telemedicine & video consultations
✅ Parent-child checkup examinations
✅ Diagnostics: X-ray, CT, MRI, ultrasound, blood tests, etc.
✅ Outpatient surgical procedures (not eye laser)
✅ Preventive checkups: urological, gynecological, ophthalmological, dermatological
✅ Prescribed medications, homeopathic remedies
✅ Prescription fees (100% with Austrian statutory insurance)
✅ Vaccinations including travel vaccinations

**Start does NOT cover:**
❌ Physiotherapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy
❌ Psychotherapy, clinical psychology, health psychology
❌ Medical aids, crutches, bandages, braces
❌ Glasses, contact lenses
❌ Refractive eye surgery (laser eye surgery)
❌ Osteopathy, medical massage, healing massage
❌ Dietetics, music therapy
❌ Dental/oral surgery, prosthetics
❌ Cosmetic/aesthetic procedures

**Optimal ADDS to Start (additional coverage up to €1,400/year):**
✅ Therapies & treatments: physiotherapy, psychotherapy, osteopathy, etc. (€560/yr)
✅ Medical aids, glasses, contact lenses, refractive eye surgery (€280/yr + €280 additional/2yr)
✅ Higher medication coverage (€560 vs. €280/yr)
✅ Higher doctor coverage (€1,400 vs. €1,120/yr)

### Worldwide Coverage

All Privatarzt tariffs provide coverage **worldwide** (weltweiter Versicherungsschutz). The tariff provisions explicitly state this: "Der Versicherungsschutz umfasst weltweit Kostenersatz für Leistungen für medizinisch notwendige ambulante Heilbehandlungen" — coverage extends to medically necessary outpatient treatments worldwide.
